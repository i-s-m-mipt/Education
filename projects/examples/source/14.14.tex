\textbf{Amdahl's Law}

\bigskip

Let $T$ is serial part and $1 - T$ is parallel part

\bigskip

Let $N$ is total number of processors

\bigskip

Let $S$ is theoretical speedup

\bigskip

Hence, $S(N) = 1\,/\,(T + (1\,-\,T)\,/\,N)$ and $\lim{S(N)} = 1\,/\,T$ when $N \rightarrow \infty$

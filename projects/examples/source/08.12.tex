\textbf{Karatsuba Fast Multiplication Algorithm}

\bigskip

Let $x = x_2 \cdot 10^N + x_1$, $y = y_2 \cdot 10^N + y_1$, where $x_i,\,y_i \in I\,(\,N\,)$ for $i \in \{\,1,\,2\,\}$

\bigskip

Hence, $z = x \cdot y = x_2 \cdot y_2 \cdot 10^{2N} + (x_2 \cdot y_1 + x_1 \cdot y_2) \cdot 10^N + x1 \cdot y1$ with time complexity $O(N^2)$

\bigskip

Let $a = x_2 \cdot y_2$, $b = x_1 \cdot y_1$, $c = (x_2 + x_1) \cdot (y_2 + y_1)$

\bigskip

Hence, $x_2 \cdot y_1 + x_1 \cdot y_2 = (x_2 + x_1) \cdot (y_2 + y_1) - x_1 \cdot y_1 - x_2 \cdot y_2 = c - b - a$

\bigskip

Hence, $z = x \cdot y = a \cdot 10^{2N} + (c - b - a) \cdot 10^N + b$ with time complexity $O(N^{\log_{2}3})\approx O(N^{1.585})$
